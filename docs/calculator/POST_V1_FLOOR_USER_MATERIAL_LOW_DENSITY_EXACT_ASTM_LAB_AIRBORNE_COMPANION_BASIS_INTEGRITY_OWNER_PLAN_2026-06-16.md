## Owner Scope

Implement only the bounded low-density exact ASTM lab-airborne companion
basis owner:

- promote `Rw` and `C` to the lightweight-family floor-system companion
  when the request is the same custom low-density exact ASTM floor route;
- keep exact ASTM `AIIC` / `IIC` basis ownership unchanged;
- keep ISO impact companions `Ln,w`, `DeltaLw`, `L'n,w`, `L'nT,w`,
  and `L'nT,50` unchanged;
- keep building airborne outputs `R'w`, `Dn,w`, `Dn,A`, `DnT,w`, and
  `DnT,A` unchanged;
- keep `STC` and `Ctr` outside this owner unless a real companion basis
  exists for them.

## Required Inputs

The owner must require the same physical boundary as the closed
low-density exact ASTM slices:

- custom low-density concrete base / visible floating-floor stack;
- exact ASTM E492/E1007 bands rated through ASTM E989;
- low-density floor impact context with load basis and resilient-layer
  dynamic stiffness;
- complete field or building context when field/building outputs are
  requested;
- finite floor-system ratings on
  `predictor_lightweight_concrete_family_estimate`.

## Negative Boundaries

Do not:

- promote generic `IIC` / `AIIC` without exact ASTM bands;
- infer `STC` from `Rw` or from `Rw + C`;
- infer `Ctr` when the companion semantic is `Rw + C`;
- source-crawl or retune formulas;
- widen every lightweight-family floor to use lab-airborne companions
  unless the low-density exact ASTM route boundary is proven;
- add frontend or route-input changes.
