  `needs_input`, and lab/impact unsupported boundaries.

  source-absent budget tightening.

  bounded anchors, or holdout evidence. They must not tighten field or
  building values from lab-only proximity.
- Spectrum companions such as `C` / `Ctr` may be selected only if the
  route owns the required spectrum or standard adapter; otherwise they
  stay unsupported or become a research prerequisite.

Iteration 3: choose current-gate reconciliation rather than a premature runtime owner.

- The latest full current gate still exits non-zero on 34 known stale
  metric/basis assertions after the opening/leak apparent companion

- The failure classes include impact routes expecting airborne
  `Rw`/`C`/`Ctr` support, opening/leak assertions expecting building

  resolver precedence expectations that predate

  expectations are reconciled, because the gate cannot otherwise
