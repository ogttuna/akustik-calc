- exact field ASTM `AIIC 50` stays on the ASTM E989 basis;
- same-stack lightweight ISO companions stay on
  `predictor_lightweight_concrete_family_estimate` /
  `predictor_lightweight_concrete_delta_lw_dynamic_improvement_estimate`;
- direct+flanking field context calculates `L'n,w 66.1`;
- room-volume standardization calculates `L'nT,w 63.7`;
- explicit `ci50_2500Db 3` calculates `L'nT,50 66.7`;
- complete building context keeps `R'w 51`, `Dn,w 51`, `Dn,A 50.2`,
  `DnT,w 54`, and `DnT,A 52.6`.

- do not reuse ASTM E1007 field bands as direct+flanking source curves;
- do not publish opposite ASTM `IIC` from field `AIIC` input;

  is missing;

- do not retune formulas or import source rows.
